Durak - Game Rules

Basic Rules

Durak (Russian for "fool") is a traditional card game where the objective is to get rid of all your cards. The last player with cards becomes the "durak" (fool).

Setup

- Players receive 6 cards each

- One card is turned face up to determine the trump suit

- The player with the lowest trump card attacks first

Gameplay

- The attacker plays a card to attack

- The defender must beat it with a higher card of the same suit or any trump

- If the defender succeeds, the attacker can play another card of a rank already on the table

- Up to 6 attack cards can be played in a round

- If the defender fails, they must take all cards in play

- If all attacks are defended, cards are discarded and the defender becomes the next attacker

Perevodnoy Variant

If enabled, a defender can "pass" an attack to the next player by playing a card of the same rank. The defender then becomes the attacker.

End of Game

After the deck is empty, players who run out of cards leave the game. The last player with cards is the "durak".
